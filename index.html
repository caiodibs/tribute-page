Alberto Santos Dumont

The aviation pioneer

Timeline

  • 1873 Born on the 20 of July in the Brazilian town of Palmira. He was the youngest of seven children.
  • 1891 He moved to France.
  • 1892-1896 He studied chemistry, physics, mechanics, and electricity. He used to return to Brazil for holidays.
  • 1897 He began his plans to design and construct balloons.
  • 1898 The M1, was his first balloon. It not worked as intended, and almost caused his death.
  • 1899 The M2, a better design with few additions. At the same year, he constructed the M3, a bigger and better version than the previous.
  • 1900-1901 He made the M4 and M5 with the intent of win the Deutsch tournament. He made several improvements and made impressive innovations.
  • 1901 He won the Deutsch tournament with the M6 on the 19 of October. he distributed the prize between his team and unemployed people at Paris. He also received international recognition and received the title of Dirigible's inventor
  • 1905 Built his first sailplane.
  • 1906 On the 12 of November, he made 6 public demonstrations flying with his plane. It was the first time that a flight was filmed.
  • 1910 He decided to retire.
  • 1914 He saw his dreams becoming nightmares, sinse his planes started to be used as a military weapon.
  • 1922 During the commemorations of the 100th anniversary of Brazil's independency, he made a flight from Rio de Janeiro to SÃ£o Paulo.
  • 1932 Was the year of his death.

Curiosities

  • Rio de Janeiro Intarnational airport was named Santos Dumont in 1916
  • On the 31 of June, 1932, the town of Palmira was renamed to Santos Dumont as a tribute to his deeds.
  • There are two sculptures of Santos Dumont in France.
  • Santos Dumont have had his name inscribed on a lunar crater.
